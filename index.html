Who We Are

Chitkala® is a pioneering experimental learning platform, recreational activity centre, and co-creation space.

We see that every young learner is naturally curious, active, and creative. We recognize this immense potential in every young learner and aim to cultivate it through a unique approach.

We focus on bringing them back to hands-on, activity-based learning while complementing digital platforms, aligning with NEP-2020 and NCF-2023.

Our Philosophy

🔬

Experiential Learning

Bringing learners back to hands-on, activity-based discovery away from pure theory.

🧠

Critical Thinking

Encouraging problem-solving and analytical skills from an early age for lifelong adaptation.

Constructive Habits
